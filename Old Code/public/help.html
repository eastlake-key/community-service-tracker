Help



Eastlake High School's Community Service Portal exists to make tracking your service hours easier. Hours can be tracked for Key Club and for school recognition.

If it's your first time here, make sure to start by creating an account on the home page.

If you've already created an account, log in using the button in the menu to access the tracking form.

Have questions about your community service hours? Email us at eastlakecommunityservice@gmail.com
